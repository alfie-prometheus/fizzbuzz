Fizz Buzz App

A fun and interactive way to count from 1 to 100! Watch as numbers transform: multiples of 3 become Fizz, multiples of 5 turn into Buzz, and those special numbers that are multiples of both? They display as FizzBuzz. Dive in and enjoy this classic coding challenge brought to life!

Instructions

Enter a starting value and an ending value. Press the value and the values will appear.
